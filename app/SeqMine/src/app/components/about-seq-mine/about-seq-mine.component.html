Welcome to our SeqMine, a tool for our workers to upload and manage their database of sequence files. Sequence files contain sequences of DNA, which are a critical part of our research and investigation work.

Features

  • Upload and store sequence files securely on our server.
  • Search and filter sequence files by keywords, date, and file type.
  • Download or share individual sequence files with coworkers.
  • Track the history of changes made to the sequence files and their metadata.

Benefits

  • Streamline the process of uploading and managing sequence files, saving time and resources.
  • Ensure data security by keeping all sequence files in a secure and centralized location.
  • Easily collaborate with coworkers by sharing or downloading sequence files and their metadata.
  • Facilitate data tracking and analysis by keeping a history of changes to the sequenceng files and their metadata.

Getting Started

To get started with our SeqMine, simply log in with your provided credentials. If you have any questions or issues, please reach out to our support team for assistance.
